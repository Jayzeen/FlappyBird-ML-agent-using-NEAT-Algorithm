NEAT ALGORITHM  (NeuroEvolution of Augmenting Topologies)

Inputs   --> Bird Y, Top pipe, Bottom pipe, Bottom

Outputs  --> Jump up or not (2 outputs)

Activation function --> TanH (Value between -1 and 1)

Population size  --> 100(In this case 100 birds)

Fitness function  -->  distance travelled by a birds(Higher the distance better the bird)

Max generations -->  30
